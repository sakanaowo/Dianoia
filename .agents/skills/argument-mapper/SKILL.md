---

When mapping an argument:

1. Separate claim, evidence, assumption, implication, and objection.
2. Steelman the strongest version before criticizing it.
3. Identify hidden assumptions.
4. Identify what evidence would strengthen or weaken the claim.
5. Include counterarguments.
6. Avoid presenting controversial claims as settled facts.
7. Prefer clear Vietnamese explanations.
8. Link to relevant domains and themes.

Recommended note structure:

```md
---
type: argument
status: growing
domains:
  - "[[...]]"
themes:
  - "[[...]]"
created: YYYY-MM-DD
---

# Title

## Luận điểm

## Lập luận ủng hộ

## Lập luận phản đối

## Giả định ngầm

## Điểm yếu

## Ví dụ thực tế

## Liên kết
```
